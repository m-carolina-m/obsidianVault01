Dog tricks:
-Sitz(siitz like fits=sit)
Signal: hand palm facing front
Way to teach: move treat over the head 
-Bleib!(blype=stay)
Signal: hand Palm facing front
Way to teach: in sitting position make signal and wait. Reward only if dog doesn't move. Try moving backwards little by little. 
After mastering sitting position can be teach from down position
-Platz(plahtz like cats=down)
Signal: move hand Palm Facing front from up to down
Way to teach: in sitting position move treat down in middle of paws the to the front away from dog
-Hier(hee er=come)
signal:elbow raised hand Palm facing us moving from elbow toward us
Way to teach: with a leash pull towards us. Little by little move away using longer leash. Finally try without a leash
-Gib pföte(gipp fote=shake hands linke=left  rechte=right)
Signal:hand sideway with too fingers, can be taught right and left
Way to teach:begin in sitting position. With treat in hand lower to the floor and encourage puppy to touch it with the paw. Raise hand little by little until puppy is raising hand to chest. Finally put treat behind you make signal and extend hand to receive paw and shake. Repeat everything with other side
